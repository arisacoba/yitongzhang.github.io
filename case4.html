bundl

Smart devices purport to make our home life more simple, but busy homeowners quickly become saturated by the large amount of settings and toggles. As part of designation, I was challenged with designing a universal remote control app for smart home. Our solution, BUNDL, focused on merging settings and devices into toggle groups, enabling users to make use of all their devices in concert, with never more than 2 clicks.
