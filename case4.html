To better incorporate type in my design, I need to develop a large working repertoire of fonts in my mind. But memorizing a bunch of fonts is bad because it limits my exploration to the size of my memory and makes me over-reliant on sites like Typewolf and Fonts In Use. To solve this problem, I'm making FontSearch, an app that lets users search Google Fonts not by their name, but by their features.
