crowdgig

Crowdgig is a job listing site with a twist: you get paid to find yourself or your friends a job. The company had a great idea, but no design experience so they hired me to make their landing page and develop the beginning of an identity. My approach was to combine imagery and illustration to reflect the part-software, part-service nature of the company.
