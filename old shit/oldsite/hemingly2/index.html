Hemingly

Goodbye for now...

Over the past year, we at Hemingly have worked hard to make a great service for companies to easily get their time consuming work done. Although we succeeded in many ways, we also struggled and faced a lot of obstacles, some of which cannot be overcome without putting significant strain on our personal lives. That is why we’ve shut down the company as of Nov 27, 2015.

We feel incredibly fortunate to have worked with amazing customers and cannot thank you all for supporting us on this incredible journey.

Until next time, feel free to reach out to us at founders@hemingly.com

Charlie, Mathieu & Yitong
