Aptamigo

The client is a luxury apartment listing site that aims to reduce the average apartment search time from 20 hours to 2. We were challenged with designing a visit booking system that simultaneously let renters easily book tours, while also funneling quality leads to leasing offices. To accomplish this, we created a comprehensive filtering system that let renters quickly hone in on listings that fit their needs.
