About Yitong

Work is hard. Teamwork is harder. In fact, life in general is pretty hard. As a product designer, I embrace that because I believe in design’s potential to untangle the hardest problems around us. Good design carries us over the hurdles and enables us to live better, more productive lives — with our friends, at work, and beyond.

While my passion lies in UI and UX, I love messing with code to bring my ideas to life. And as a former startup founder, I’m comfortable relentlessly iterating from UI → UX → code in search of a better solution. See more of my story →
