About Yitong

Getting a cab in NYC is hard. Teamwork is hard. In fact, life in general is pretty hard. As a product designer, I embrace that. Because I believe in design’s potential to untangle the hardest and hairiest problems around us. Good design carries us over the hurdles and enables us to live better, fuller lives — with our friends, at work and beyond.

While my passion lies in UI and UX, I love messing with code to bring my ideas to life. And as a former startup founder, I’m comfortable relentlessly iterating from UI -> UX -> code in search of a better solution.
